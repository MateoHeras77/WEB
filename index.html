Skills in Data Analysis, AI, Econometrics, and More

  • Data Analysis and Exploration

    Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) allows us to understand the general situation of the variables necessary to make informed decisions (customers, accounts, products, markets, etc). It helps identify areas of improvement and opportunities in the market.

  • Machine Learning and Econometric Models

    Machine learning models and econometric analysis help uncover relationships, probabilities, and the level of influence of variables on each other. This enhances decision-making processes.

    Time series analysis helps to understand how specific variables (economic or business-related, such as GDP, CPI, sales, etc.) fluctuate over time, allowing for future value forecasting.
